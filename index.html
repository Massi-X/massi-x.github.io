Nice to meet you, surfer of the web.

Let me introduce myself: I am Massi-X and I'm an all-purpose developer, infact even if my main job is not about programming at all, I do love it! I began learning how to develop in the teenage all by myself and then slowly approached new challenges to keep improving my skills. But what about my everyday life? Well usually I wake up, run into some problems that bores me, fix it with some developing! Let's be serious now, I really do find problems in the computer world and I try my best to improve them, be with a new App/Script/Module, be with a contribution to open source projects. And that's really the reason why you won't find a straight path there: my projects skip from Android apps to FreePBX modules to small scripts that fixes sall annoiances. Another thing that distingueshes my work is the search for perfection: I do really like to make things the best I could putting the UI in front of everything followed by a strong backend, and keep out all the headaches that sometimes are characteristic of some solutions!

And if you are interested to learn which languages I have experience with here is a list: Java/Kotlin, PHP, HTML, CSS, Javascript and a little of Python and C++.
